A unified platform for
disability welfare in India.

Digiability brings welfare schemes, document support, health camps, insurance and information together on a single, accessible digital platform — connecting NGOs, service providers, and beneficiaries.

Our Services

What we're building

Five core service areas — designed to digitise every step of the disability welfare journey, from discovery to delivery.

Welfare Schemes

Discover and apply for government and private welfare schemes — eligibility matching, paperless applications, real-time status tracking.

Document Support

Apply for UDID, disability certificates and other essential documents — guided forms, document upload, end-to-end processing.

Health Camps

Find upcoming health, diagnostic, and assistive-device camps near you — register, get reminders, and access camp reports.

Insurance Schemes

Browse and enrol in insurance products tailored for Persons with Disabilities — premium tracking, policy documents, claim support.

Information Center

Curated knowledge base — your rights, available benefits, application guides, and FAQs in your preferred language.

One Connected Ecosystem

NGOs, service providers, field officers, and beneficiaries — all working on a single, unified platform with end-to-end visibility.
